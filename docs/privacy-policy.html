Privacy Policy — Saksham Homoeopathy

Last updated: June 2026

Saksham Homoeopathy (“we”, “our”, “the clinic”) operates the Saksham Homoeopathy mobile application for existing patients and clinic staff. This policy explains what we collect, why we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Phone number — used to sign in with a one-time password (OTP) via Firebase Authentication.
  • Profile information — name and profile photo you provide.
  • Appointments — booking date, time slot, and patient details you enter for visits.
  • Chat messages — messages between you and clinic staff in the in-app chat.
  • Treatment history — medicine and history records associated with your phone number as entered by authorised clinic staff.
  • Photos — images you choose from your device for your profile picture, stored in Firebase Storage.
  • Push notification token — if you allow notifications, we store a device token to send appointment and message alerts.

How we use information

We use your data to:

  • Authenticate you and manage your account;
  • Schedule and manage clinic appointments;
  • Enable communication with the clinic;
  • Display your treatment history as recorded by the clinic;
  • Send service-related notifications;
  • Protect our services (including Firebase App Check).

Third-party services

We use Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Storage, Cloud Functions, and App Check) to host and process app data. Firebase processes data according to Google’s privacy documentation.

Data retention

We retain information while your account is active and as needed for clinic operations and legal obligations. When you delete your account in the app, we delete your Firebase Authentication account and associated profile, bookings, notifications, chat threads, history records linked to your phone number, and profile photos, subject to technical limits and backups that may persist for a limited period.

Your rights and account deletion

You may update your profile in the app. To delete your account and associated app data, use Profile → Delete account or email support@sakshamhomoeopathy.com.

Security

We use industry-standard measures including encrypted connections and access controls. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. Parents may enter dependent details for appointments under their own account.

Medical disclaimer

The app supports booking and communication with Saksham Homoeopathy. It does not provide emergency services or replace in-person medical care.

Contact

Questions about this policy: support@sakshamhomoeopathy.com
