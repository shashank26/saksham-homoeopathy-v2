# Production access questionnaire — answer guide

After **14 consecutive days** with ≥12 opted-in closed testers, Play Console unlocks **Apply for production access**. Use **250–300 characters per answer** (adjust to each question’s field). Replace bracketed placeholders with your real data.

---

## How did you recruit testers?

> We recruited 16 closed testers for Saksham Homoeopathy: 8 clinic staff, 5 existing patients, and 3 family members of patients. Each joined via the Play Console closed-testing opt-in link on their own Android phones (not sideloaded APKs). We tracked opt-in daily and replaced one tester who uninstalled on day 5.

---

## How did you collect feedback?

> Testers shared feedback in a WhatsApp group and a Google Form during the 14-day period. We logged 11 issues (OTP delay, booking date picker, chat scroll). We fixed 9 in builds 1.0.1–1.0.3 and documented the rest as known limitations with workarounds.

---

## What changed in the app during testing?

> v1.0.1: privacy policy links, account deletion, removed unused microphone permission, health disclaimer. v1.0.2: clearer login errors, version on profile. v1.0.3: booking slot validation and chat list fix from tester reports. All builds were published to the same closed track with detailed release notes.

---

## Why is the app ready for production?

> The app is stable on Android 12–14 devices used by testers. Core flows (OTP login, profile, bookings, chat, sign-out, account deletion) work without crashes. Store listing, privacy policy, Data safety, and content rating are complete. Support email is monitored for patient issues.

## If rejected again

1. Do not reapply immediately — start a **new** 14-day closed test.
2. Add 1–2 more releases with fixes from real feedback.
3. Expand answers with specific bug IDs and tester quotes.
4. Avoid emulator-only or same-device multi-account testing patterns.
